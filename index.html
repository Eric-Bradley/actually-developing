My Steak Recipe

This steak is as good as it gets. With a slightly unconventional techniques for ensuring tenderness that makes some people a little angry.

Ingredients

  • 10oz Strip Steak
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic Powder
  • Avocado Oil

Instructions

  • Using a meat hammer, tenderize your steak.
  • Season steak with salt, pepper, and garlic powder.
  • Allow to sit at room temperature for 15 minutes
  • Pour or spray avocado oil onto the pan and heat pan to medium heat.
  • Place steak on pan and allow to cook for 4 minutes and 30 seconds.
  • Flip the steak and allow it cook for another 4 minutes and 30 seconds.
  • Remove steak from pan and immidiately top with butter.
  • Allow the steak to sit for 8 minutes.
  • ENJOY!
