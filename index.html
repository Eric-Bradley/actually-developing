My Steak Recipe

This steak is as good as it gets. With a slightly unconventional technique for ensuring tenderness that makes some people a little ANGRY.

Ingredients

  • 10oz Strip Steak
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Garlic Powder
  • Avocado Oil

Instructions

  1. Using a meat hammer, tenderize your steak.
  2. Season steak with salt, pepper, and garlic powder.
  3. Allow to sit at room temperature for 15 minutes
  4. Pour or spray avocado oil onto the pan and heat pan to medium heat.
  5. Place steak on pan and allow to cook for 4 minutes and 30 seconds.
  6. Flip the steak and allow it cook for another 4 minutes and 30 seconds.
  7. Remove steak from pan and immidiately top with butter.
  8. Allow the steak to sit for 8 minutes.
  9. ENJOY!
